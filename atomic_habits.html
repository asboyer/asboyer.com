Atomic Habits

Andrew Boyer's notes

Introductions

After seeing this book circulate through the self-improvement side of the internet for quite some time, I finally decided to give in to the hype and read James Clear's Atomic Habits. These are my personal notes and thoughts on the book.

If you want to read the book yourself, I provided the link to buy it on Amazon, among other links that I found relevant to the book. Take a look at both of those Thomas Frank videos, they give a helpful and visual summary of the points Clear makes throughout his book.

I began reading this book in mid-May of 2021, but decided to start over and take some detailed notes after I realized that Clear was the real deal. The lessons and advice presented in the book I have found both thoughtful and practical, and I feel that it would be a missed opportunity if I didn't write down some key points for future reference. The goal is to actually act on the valuable practices and advice Clear gives, not just read it once and forget. I hope these notes will provide a solid understanding and review of the important points in this book.
